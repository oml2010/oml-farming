About OML Farming

OML Farming is a community-focused pig farming enterprise based in Nkhata Bay District, Malawi. We specialize in pork production and pig feed manufacturing, combining modern agricultural practices with local knowledge to drive food security, employment, and sustainable development.

What We Do

Pork Production

We raise healthy pigs under hygienic and humane conditions, supplying fresh pork to local markets, butcheries, and institutions.

Pig Feed Production

We produce affordable, nutritious pig feed using locally sourced ingredients to support smallholder and commercial farmers.

Farmer Support

Training, advisory services, and market linkages for local pig farmers to improve productivity and incomes.

Key Impact Indicators (KPIs)

  • 50+ jobs created for youth and women (target: Year 3)
  • 300+ smallholder farmers supported with feed & training
  • 120 tons of pork produced annually
  • 25% increase in household income among beneficiary farmers
  • 30% reduction in feed costs through local sourcing

Community & Development Impact

Job Creation

Employment opportunities for youth and women in pig production, feed processing, and farm management.

Food & Nutrition Security

Increased availability of affordable, high-quality protein for households in Nkhata Bay.

Local Economic Growth

Supporting local suppliers and farmers through inclusive agribusiness value chains.

Sustainability & Green Practices

Our operations align with donor and NGO sustainability priorities through responsible manure management, composting, efficient water use, integrated crop–livestock systems, and planned biogas production to reduce emissions and energy costs.

Partnerships & Donor Engagement

OML Farming welcomes partnerships with NGOs, development agencies, and donors supporting youth employment, climate-smart agriculture, food security, and rural development. We are committed to transparency, accountability, and measurable social impact.

Focus Areas: Job creation • Climate-smart agriculture • SME development • Nutrition • Community resilience

Development Approach (GIZ-Aligned)

Value Chain Development

Strengthening the entire pig farming value chain from production, feed supply, processing, to market access to ensure sustainability and profitability.

Capacity Building

Training smallholder farmers, youth, and women in modern pig farming, agribusiness skills, and financial literacy.

Climate-Smart Agriculture

Promoting manure management, biogas systems, and efficient resource use to reduce environmental impact.

Gender & Youth Inclusion

OML Farming prioritizes inclusive development by ensuring at least 40% participation of women and strong involvement of youth in employment, training, and enterprise opportunities. Equal access to resources, training, and market opportunities is embedded in our implementation model.

Monitoring & Evaluation Framework

Our Monitoring & Evaluation system follows results-based management principles aligned with GIZ standards.

  • Regular data collection on production, income, and employment
  • Quarterly performance reviews
  • Annual impact assessments
  • Transparent reporting to partners and stakeholders
